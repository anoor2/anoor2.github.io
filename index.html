How to Knit for Beginners

Knitting is as Simple as 1, 2, 3

Knitting boils down to three essential skills. Master them and you’re officially a knitter. It’s that simple!

The Three Skill Are:

  • Cast On
  • Knit Stitch
  • Cast Off

Supplies to Gather

Yarn: Chunky yarn because it is easier to knit with for beginners.

Needles: Wood or Bamboo needles that are thick!
