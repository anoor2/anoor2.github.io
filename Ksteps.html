Knit Stitch

Step 1: Hold the needle with the cast on stitches in your left hand, and the empty needle in your right hand.

Step 2: Hold the empty Needle in front of everything in your right hand

Hold the empty needle in front of the working yarn.

Step 3: Insert the Empty Needle into the First Stitch

Insert the new needle into the first stitch, going upwards into the stitch and creating an “X” with your needles.

Step 4: Wrap the Yarn Around the Needle

Wrap the yarn around the new needle only in a counter clockwise direction.

Step 5: Use the Right Needle to pull under the left needle

Step 6: Repeat
